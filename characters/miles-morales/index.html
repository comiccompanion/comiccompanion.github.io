Miles Morales

Miles Morales’ spider powers are significantly different from his counterparts in other dimensions. Not as skilled in web-slinging, movement isn’t his strong suit. However, he can blend into his surroundings in order to evade all attacks, or use his electrical Venom Strike to neutralize close enemies. He can even wrap the Villain in his web, delaying its turn and buying the Heroes more time.
