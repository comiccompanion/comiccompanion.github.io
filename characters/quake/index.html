Quake

Here to shake things up, Quake uses her ability to generate localized earthquakes to attack multiple targets in any Location around her she chooses. A well-trained Agent of S.H.I.E.L.D., her skills as a spy allow her to see the next Master Plan card and possibly place it at the bottom of the deck whenever she ends her turn together with a Henchman ready to spill the beans. Finally, she’s an expert sniper, dealing a lethal attack across the play area as long as she doesn’t move.
