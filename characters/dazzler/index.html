Dazzler

Dazzler may not be a natural fighter, but she’s certainly not one to hide in the shadows. Light and sound are the fuels that keeps her going, so she’ll always seek a captivated audience for her heroic performances. Her sound conversion gives her Attack tokens equal to the number of Civilians in her Location, while her Light Projection grants her Heroic Action tokens equal to the number of Thugs around her.
