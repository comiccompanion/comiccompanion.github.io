Domino

Domino wouldn’t be the renowned mercenary she is if she weren’t such a master assassin, very adaptable and able to deal a devastating killing blow when the time comes. But without a doubt her most remarkable characteristic is her probability manipulation powers, which bring her uncanny good luck. When she’s around, all of a sudden Overflows don’t happen, Henchmen miss their attacks, and even Villains become late for their appointments.
