Ant Man

Scott Lang, the Ant-Man, proves that the greatest things may come in the smallest sizes. As tiny as the Ant-Man can become, nothing can diminish his will to do good. His determination allows him to perform several Heroic Actions. But most impressive is his ability to alter his size, becoming so small to the point where he is nigh-invisible, becoming invulnerable to attacks , or to suddenly grow to gigantic proportions, taking enemies by surprise and smacking them hard.
