Captain Carter

Captain Carter’s leadership inspires her teammates to persevere through any adversity, giving them Wild tokens and recovering their hand of cards. She is also very resourceful in the field, scrounging for Action tokens of any kind needed at the moment. Coupled with her Super Soldier Serum-enhanced abilities, she is trained to use her shield as a ranged weapon, being able to bounce it back again and again.

Captain Carter’s Shield Equipment cards allows her to use it to either attack an adjacent Location or to protect the Heroes in her Location.
