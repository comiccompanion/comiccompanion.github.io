Hela

Hela starts the game banished, not occupying any Location, and will only show up when it’s time for her to finally strike at the Heroes. Her master plan involves bringing about Ragnarok, the end times, by knocking out all heroes at the same time. To do so, she not only lashes out at the heroes in epic fashion, but also raises an undead army throughout the Locations, distributing Threat cards that equip them with deadly Necroswords. But chief among her Henchmen is the Fenris Wolf. This giant beast keeps Hela protected as long as it’s alive, and Hela in turn is able to not only heal it but bring it back from the dead!

Hela brings a completely new dynamic to the game, offering a relentless challenge to players not intimidated by a little extra complexity.
