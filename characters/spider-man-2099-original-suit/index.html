Spider Man 2099 (Original Suit)

Donning his original suit, Spider-Man 2099 has a Starting Hand card that gives him the use of the Spinnerets on his arms, ensuring he always has a Move at his disposal, even when the previous Hero card in the Storyline offers him none. His accelerated vision allows him to look into the next Master Plan card. Then, he can deal with his enemies either using the powerful attacks of his talons and fangs, or he can tangle them in webs, cancelling their next BAM effect.
