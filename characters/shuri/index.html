Shuri

Shuri is a fierce defender of her homeland, whose genius is hard to match. She’s always ready to perform a Heroic Action, favoring more of a support role instead of the front lines. As such, she can use her inventions to not only grant her allies with an extra Wild token to perform any action, but also replenish their hand, thus saving them from being KO’d and setting them up for devastating turns.
