Howard the Duck

In a world full of Super Heroes with special powers, there’s nothing really special about Howard. Except, of course, for the fact that he is a talking duck living in a world ruled by hairless apes ahem humans. At least he wears pants! Don’t be so quick to dismiss him, though. Compared to humans’ average intelligence, Howard is the smartest duck you will ever find, and as an expert in the art of Quack-fu and packing big and experimental weapons, such as the Neutron Desintegrator, he’s able to deal a great amount of damage to enemies, so much that he can even bring enemies to their last health with a single shot.
