Darkstar

As a Hero, Darkstar uses her mystical command of the Darkforce to perform amazing feats. She can open portals to teleport anywhere, whenever she wishes. She can also create Darkforce constructs to perform extra Attack or Heroic actions across multiple location. Darkstar is also part of the soviet Winter Guard, so her and other Heroes may gain extra Heroic Action tokens when she plays her Winter Guard card.
