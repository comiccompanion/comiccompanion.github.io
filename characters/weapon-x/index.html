Weapon X

Weapon X is a Hero of pure unbridled rage. Almost all of his cards allow him to attack in an unstoppable fury, with his freshly forged adamantium claws doing a lot of extra damage. His starting hand card gives him the ability to ignore 1 damage every Villain turn. However, if he does so, he must play his next card randomly, as his berserker rage blinds him.
