Bishop

With his Energy Resistance starting hand card, Bishop is completely safe from any attack made at him from adjacent Locations. His ability to absorb energy not only allows him to ignore some of the damage he would receive, but he can channel that absorbed energy into extra Attack tokens he can use to fight back.
