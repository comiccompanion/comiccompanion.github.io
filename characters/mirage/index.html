Mirage

Dani Moonstar is a truly versatile Hero, with a varied array of powers. Using her ability to cast empathic illusions of others’ fears or desires, she can easily manipulate them, relocating Civilians, Thugs, and even Henchmen around her. On the attack front, her Neural Arrows can shoot down enemies in adjacent Locations. And she can even take on death itself, protecting herself and other Heroes around her from being KO’d.
