War Machine

War Machine makes use of his advanced military training and cutting-edge battle suit to become the most efficient fighting agent on the field. He’s extremely mobile and possesses a wide array of attack options. Be it carpet-bombing his location, firing a rocket at range, or using hit-and-run tactics, he doesn’t bring his arsenal for nothing.
