Wiccan

One of the Scarlet Witch’s magical children, Wiccan is a powerful warlock in his own right. This Young Avenger can fire force blasts at adjacent locations or warp reality to allow another Hero swap cards with the Storyline. His wild magic can generate several different effects, from moving to any location, allowing others to draw cards, to revealing the Villain’s next Master Plan.

Forged from a Kree Nega-Band, the Wedding Rings worn by Wiccan and his husband, the Hulkling, form a powerful bond between them. They can be used to allow the wearer to draw an extra card in their time of need.
