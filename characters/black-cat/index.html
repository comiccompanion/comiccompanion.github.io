Black Cat

Black Cat’s lawless days are behind her, though she’s still learning the ropes of this Super Hero business. She’s not the most apt at Heroic Actions, but she always has a trick or two up her sleeves. Her Cat Instincts allow her to accumulate all kinds of Action tokens for the right occasion. More useful for the team as a whole is her ability to influence the Masterplan deck. Whether it’s just her luck, or she’s jinxing the Villain, she’s able to postpone Masterplan cards that would be too devastating.
