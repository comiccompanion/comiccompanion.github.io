Ch'od

Ch’od is the muscle of the Starjammers. One of his starting hand cards allows him to add Water tokens to 3 locations. As an amphibious creature, he is in his element in those locations. When fighting there he may also gain Wild tokens and he can even hide away in the water, evading all attacks. Besides a strong body, Ch’od also has a strong mind, gifted with telepathic immunity that shields him from Crisis tokens.

The Starjammer crew are a closely-knit gang. Whenever Ch’od plays one of his Starjammer Crew cards, he can perform an extra Wild Action if the previous card in the Storyline is also some type of Starjammer card.
