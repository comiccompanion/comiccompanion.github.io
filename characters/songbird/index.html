Songbird

Songbird can manipulate sound like no other. Her sonic blasts are able to strike both adjacent locations at the same time. She can also manipulate sound to create a solid defensive shield capable of protecting all Heroes in her location. Her Manipulation Song can turn the minds of Henchmen, making them attack Villains or other Henchmen instead.
