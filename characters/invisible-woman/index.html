Invisible Woman

Sue’s control of her invisibility allows her to use it reactively to great effect. Her starting hand card keeps her on alert, so that whenever a Master Plan card is revealed she may choose to go invisible and avoid any damage that turn. However, that forces her to play her next card facedown, only benefitting from her teammate’s actions. She can also use her psionic force fields in many creative ways, granting her extra Wild tokens. Her Teamwork cards add Heroic Action tokens to the Team card.
