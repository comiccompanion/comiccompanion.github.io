Beast

Beast is extremely strong and agile, moving around, saving people, and striking at enemies in a constant show of force. His regenerating factor also allow him to easily recover from any damage he may take. But it is his genius intelligence that truly sets him apart. His intellect allows him to use Heroic actions to perform any sort of action he may need to.
