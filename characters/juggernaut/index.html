Juggernaut

Juggernaut is as straightforward as he is strong. This unstoppable Villain charges headlong through the Heroes, dealing damage to anyone he crosses paths with along the way. Trying to stay off his path will be a challenge, since you never know which direction he’ll charge next. His Master Plan moves as relentlessly as he does, being accelerated whenever he KO’s a Hero or causes an Overflow.

The Threats scattered over the Locations grant him the powers of Cyttorak, the demon-god that turned him into the Juggernaut. If the Heroes don’t deal with this threat, Juggernaut ignores 1 damage every Hero turn, and deals extra damage or cancels the powers of Heroes in specific Locations.
