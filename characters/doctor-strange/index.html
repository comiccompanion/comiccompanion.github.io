Doctor Strange

Doctor Strange is a Hero who walks a different path. His deck contains almost no Attack actions, but his sorcery can truly do wonders. His Hero deck contains a wide magical arsenal. He can quickly move around by either opening a dimensional portal or by letting his cloak take him there (though the cloak often prefers attacking enemies itself). The Orb of Agamotto reveals the Villain’s secrets, forcing them to always keep the top Masterplan card open. The Book of Vishanti can hinder the Villain’s plan by removing a card of your choice from the Masterplan deck. And finally, the Eye of Agamotto can buy the Heroes some time by returning a Masterplan card from the Storyline back into the deck.
