Logan

Middle-aged Logan comes from a timeline when the relentless extermination of mutants has taken its toll on him. With a starting hand card, he’s able to stand the pain and ignore 1 damage every Villain turn. Hardened by the nightmares he’s endured, Logan’s fighting experience not only grants him extra Attacks, but allows him to draw extra cards. With an increased hand size he can not only stand up to greater attacks, but becomes much more capable of adapting to any situation that comes his way.
