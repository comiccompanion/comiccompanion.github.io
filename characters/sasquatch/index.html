Sasquatch

Dr. Langkowski’s experiments made him even bigger than the Hulk, and this furry behemoth owes nothing to the big green guy in terms of destructive power. His powerful claws can cut down most enemies, and when Sasquatch goes into his “controlled fury” he lashes out at multiple targets around him. As a member of Alpha Flight, he can benefit from Alpha Team cards played by other members, gaining extra Attack tokens.
