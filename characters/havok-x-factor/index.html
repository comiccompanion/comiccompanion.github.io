Havok (X Factor)

We’ve seen Havok in the previous campaign, however that was his original version. Now, the Multiverse allows us to reconnect with Cyclops’ little brother as the experienced leader of the mutant group known as X-Factor! In this version, Havok sports not only a snazzy jacket, but also a more versatile Hero deck. With his Energy Absorption starting hand card he’s able to gain a Wild token every Villain Turn, though he’s only able to contain a single one of these at a time. He may either use these tokens normally, to do whatever task is required of him, or he can use them to power his Plasma Blast, dealing devastating damage on an adjacent Location.
