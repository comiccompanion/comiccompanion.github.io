Hope Summers

True to her name, Hope Summers brings hope to Heroes trying to vanquish the Phoenix Five. As she embraces her destiny, she can discard some of her cards in order to instantly clear Threats. She even has the power to deny the Villain, forcing them to play their next card facedown. This also removes the top card of the Master Plan deck, a dangerous move that cuts the Heroes time down.
