Spider Ham

Peter Porker is a cartoon spider bitten by a radioactive pig, which turned him into Spider-Ham. Once you get past that you’ll see he’s a loyal companion and full-fledged Super Hero, with skills that are second to none. His Toony nature can make him avoid being KO’d, trap the Villain in his web, or allow him to bend the rules of our reality to strike anywhere.
