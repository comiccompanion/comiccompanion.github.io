Throg

Throg the frog possesses the power of Thor (albeit in a pint-sized and more amphibian form). Leaping through the battle, he can move around to Attack other Locations or prove his worthiness by performing Heroic Actions. When the need is great, he may wield the mighty Frogjolnir to deal considerable damage to a target in his Location.
