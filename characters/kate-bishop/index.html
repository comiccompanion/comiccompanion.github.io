Kate Bishop

Kate Bishop is unparalleled with a bow and arrow, being able to split her shots across various locations. Clever in gaining the support of the people, she has extra Heroic Actions, gaining further Heroic tokens if she uses them to rescue Civilians. She may also get another Hero to drive her around while she shoots, moving that Hero with her and performing free Attacks in each location they enter. Resourceful, she may gain Wild tokens or recharge for free one of the Trick Arrows she always carries around.
