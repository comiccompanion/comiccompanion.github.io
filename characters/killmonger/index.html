Killmonger

Killmonger is a revolutionary out for blood and power. His strategy is to sow chaos throughout the kingdom, to the point where he’s able to overthrow king T’Challa and take the throne himself.

The core of his master plan is to gradually replace Civilians and Thugs for Crisis tokens throughout the Locations. If he manages to have the majority of Locations filled with enough Crisis, victory is his. And he’s not afraid to get his hands dirty, attacking the Heroes with vicious strikes that can easily KO them.

Killmonger’s operations leave Wakanda filled with Threats of all sorts, from assassination attempts on the Heroes, Mercenaries smuggling weapons add even more Crisis to the Locations.
