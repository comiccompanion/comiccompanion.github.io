Ghost Spider

Gwen Stacy uses her spider powers more for heroics than for direct fighting. With amazing agility, Ghost-Spider can become impossible to hit, and she can easily wipe out all the Thugs menacing a Location. Her web-weaving skills allow her to swap the position of Threat cards, altering the battlefield significantly.
