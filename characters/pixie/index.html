Pixie

Fluttering around with her fairy wings, Pixie is incredibly mobile, which is reflected in a very high number of Moves in her cards. However, that mobility is greatly enhanced by her magical powers. By using a teleportation spell, she can instantly move the whole team into a different Location. And her wings are not only good for flying, they can also release a hallucinogenic dust that can stun Villains or Henchmen, keeping them from using their BAM! while they gaze at imaginary unicorns and teddy bears.
