Dark Phoenix

Contending with the Phoenix Force, channeled through Jean Grey’s amazing powers, will be an almost insurmountable challenge. Attacks do nothing against her, only Heroic actions can hope to bring her down. And with the mental barrier Threats she puts in place, even those need to be redoubled. On the other hand, her attacks are devastating, damaging every single Hero in her Location and in both adjacent ones! And to make it worse, some of her Threats allow her attacks to bypass any form of invulnerability. With her temporal manipulation she’s able to play multiple Master Plan cards in a row, bringing the world closer to its inevitable dark fate.
