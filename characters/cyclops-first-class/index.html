Cyclops (First Class)

Cyclops is not yet the commanding leader he will one day become. At this point he’s more about barely contained energy. Working on his precision blast, he’s able to snipe a single enemy in an adjacent Location. However, he sometimes loses control of his optic blasts, laying waste to everyone around him, friend and foe alike!
