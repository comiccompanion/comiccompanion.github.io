Hercules

Hercules’ fighting prowess is the stuff of legends. Being an Olympian gives him extra Actions, while also allowing himself or his teammates to choose whether to Attack or to Move. His Almost Invulnerable Starting Hand card allows him to ignore 1 damage every Villain turn. And if even in spite of this he ends up being KO’d, his Immortal Starting Hand cards makes him come back even stronger, with an extra card in hand!
