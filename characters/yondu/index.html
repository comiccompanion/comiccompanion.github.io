Yondu

Calling Yondu a pirate with a heart of gold would be stretching it quite a bit, but he can be a Hero if the conditions are right. While his deck is mostly full of Fighting and Moving about, he may surprise you with a sudden burst of Heroic actions. His greatest asset is without a doubt his remote-controlled Yaka Arrow! It can be used to bring a Henchmen to their knees or wound all Henchmen in the game. But the real showstopper is if you manage to line up the Thugs just right. The Arrow flies around the play area, defeating one Thug in a Location, then zooming to the next one to do the same again, going round and round until it stops at a Location with no Thugs.
