Peni Parker

Peni Parker and her SP//dr suit form a very versatile crimefighter. The suit’s metal armor provides amazing protection, ignoring damage every Villain turn. On its own, SP//dr is very useful, with Robot Strength that gives extra Attacks, Webs that give extra Moves, and a Safety Net that can rescue Civilians. However, all of these features can be upgraded with a little extra charge…

By spending her limited supply of Batteries, Peni Parker can overcharge her cards, allowing Robot Strength to deal extra damage, Webs to give a free Action of her choice, and Safety Net to rescue an additional nearby Civilian.
