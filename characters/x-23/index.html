X 23

X-23 possesses many of the same abilities as her “father” including amazing fighting prowess with her claws and a powerful healing factor (like Wolverine, she has a card that must start in her hand, which allows her to heal every Villain turn). However, her advanced training sets her apart and makes her a great team player, allowing her to double the Attack symbols she gets from the previous Hero and give Move or Attack tokens to any Hero for each Heroic Action she performs.
