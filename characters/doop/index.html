Doop

What exactly Doop is remains quite a mystery. And even what he can do is often surprising. His ability to come back from incredible damage or even death is uncanny, granting him extra Wild tokens whenever he’s KO’d. Whether by cosmic, psionic, or magic ability, Doop is able to move between the margins of his universe and generate dimensional gates, popping up where you least expect. But Doop’s true passion is in being the team’s photographer and videographer. With a camera always at the ready, he makes his teammates into stars. Whenever a Hero performs a sequence of Attacks or Heroic Actions in Doop’s Location, they gain Wild tokens!
