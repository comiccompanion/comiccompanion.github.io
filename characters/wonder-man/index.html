Wonder Man

Wonder Man is an incredibly resilient Hero. Bursting with energy he is nearly immortal, since he can draw an extra card whenever he starts his turn with only 1 card in hand. Even getting to that point is unlikely for him, as a couple of his cards can keep him from taking any damage at all. Meanwhile, he uses his energy to quickly fly around, doing what must be done with extra Move and Wild Actions.
