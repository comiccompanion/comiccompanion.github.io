Sunspot

Sunspot’s a Hero who really enjoys a day in the sun. He draws power from solar energy, which grants him the ability to fly around tirelessly. Every single one of his cards allow him to move. By absorbing solar radiation, he gains extra Action tokens for each Location he visits. And he’s able to channel that solar power to do many different things, gaining Wild tokens and turning any Action tokens in his possession into Wild tokens.
