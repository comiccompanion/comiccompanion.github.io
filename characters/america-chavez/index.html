America Chavez

America Chavez is an extremely powerful Super Hero, taking on the mantle of Miss America, or simply America, to protect our reality from the ravages of evil. Her Energy Infusion can ground enemies to stardust, and she’s tough enough to become completely invulnerable from harm. Opening a Star Portal she’s able to not only teleport around, but do all manner of unexpected feats.
