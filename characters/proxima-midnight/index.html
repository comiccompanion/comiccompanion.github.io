Proxima Midnight

Proxima Midnight is a heartless fiend. She hunts down Civilians, taking them out in order to achieve her goal. The Heroes will be hard pressed to rescue Civilian lives before Proxima can get to them. Heroes can sacrifice themselves to spare Civilians, but it’s a dangerous gamble. This distraction works to her advantage, as she attempts to collect as many Infinity Stone as possible for her master.

Her Threats offer her defense against the Heroes, or tactical support in order to deal more damage against them. They can also trap Civilians, making it even harder to rescue them.

Taking on Proxima will force the Heroes to make tough decisions, as it’s never easy to watch the innocent in peril.
