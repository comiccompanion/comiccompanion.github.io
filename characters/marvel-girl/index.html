Marvel Girl

In her early days as Marvel Girl, Jean Grey still had not fully mastered her powers of telepathy, relying more on her telekinetic abilities. Manipulating objects with her mind, Marvel Girl is able to exert her influence in several places at once. Different cards are able to perform several combinations of actions in adjacent Locations.
