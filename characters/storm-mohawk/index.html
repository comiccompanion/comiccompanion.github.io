Storm (Mohawk)

The “mohawk” incarnation of Storm is different from her Core Box counterpart. She’s more focused on Heroic Actions than Movement, and is considerably more aggressive, with several cards that are capable of frying almost any enemy with a wallop of damage. Her indomitable will is also present as a starting hand card that protects her from Crisis tokens Villains may try to attach to her.
