Karnak

Karnak is gifted with foresight, with a Starting Hand card that allows him to look at the top cards of the Master Plan deck whenever he meets the Villain. His precognition may also double the Heroic symbols he uses on the previous card in the Storyline. Karnak is also a master martial artist, doubling the Attack symbols he gains from other Heroes.
