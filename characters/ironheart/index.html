Ironheart

Riri Williams is a tech genius able to make the best use of anything she has on hand, she may use Heroic Actions to perform any Action she wants or gain additional Heroic tokens. Her Ironheart suit gives her great mobility and firepower, being able to use any Action token to deal out powerful Attacks at range. She also has the aid of N.A.T.A.L.I.E., the A.I. in her suit, which can distribute Move and Attack tokens among Heroes.
