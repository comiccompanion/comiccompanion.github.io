Captain Britain (Betsy Braddock)

As Captain Britain, Betsy Braddock is a master telepathic fighter. Attuned to her enemies and ready to do her part for Excalibur, she’s able to perform a free attack whenever the Master Plan drawn contains a BAM! symbol. She’s also still a very powerful psionic combatant, able to neutralize Villains or Henchmen so they are unable to use their BAM! effects.

The Starlight Sword demands great acts of heroism, but this Equipment also rewards its wielder for them, granting an extra Attack or Heroic Action whenever Captain Britain performs 2 of those during her turn.
