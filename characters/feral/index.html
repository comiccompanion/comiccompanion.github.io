Feral

Feral is a lethal and agile predator, moving around with great ease and pouncing on its prey when they least expect. Using her claws and teeth she can do considerable damage to the poor soul she hunts down. But more than that, her acute senses help her avoid the worst perils, ignoring the first damage she would take, or blocking any Crisis token a Villain would assign to her.
