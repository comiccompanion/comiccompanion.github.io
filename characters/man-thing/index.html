Man Thing

All who fear burn at the Man-Thing’s touch!

When he transformed into the Man-Thing, Ted may have become a slow, mindless monster, but his powers make him a true force to be reckoned with. Drawing from Earth’s lifeforce he’s able to recover cards and even draw extra ones above the normal levels. His supernatural empathy pushes him to freely rescue endangered Civilians and smite threatening Thugs. He reacts instinctively to negative emotions, so if a Villain or Henchman BAMs in his Location, he automatically damages them with his acidic touch. Finally, his connection to the Nexus of All Realities allows him to not only teleport anywhere, bringing others with him, but even remove Heroes from play, replacing them with new ones!
