Mockingbird

Proficient in several forms of hand-to-hand combat and armed with her battle staves, Mockingbird brings the offense to the field. Her deck confers her good mobility and fighting capacity, while her battle staves allow her to defeat multiple Thugs at once. Better yet, her spy training allows her to thwart some Villain Masterplan cards!
