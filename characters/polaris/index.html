Polaris

Polaris inherited most of her father’s powers of magnetic manipulation, and she can use them to great effect, spreading her attacks over multiple Locations at the same time. She’s also able to create powerful magnetic fields that offer her complete protection for incoming attacks, no matter how powerful they may be.
