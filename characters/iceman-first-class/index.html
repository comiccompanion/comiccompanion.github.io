Iceman (First Class)

With a look that is more akin to snow than to ice, the original Iceman explores his icy powers in several ways, using Freeze tokens. By throwing his snow blasts he can freeze a Villain, preventing them from moving on the next turn. He can also create an ice slide in a Location, allowing all Heroes to move out of that Location without spending a Move.
