Kid Loki

If grown-up Loki is already trouble, Kid Loki is an even more unpredictable force. His deck contains an (un)godly number of Wild symbols, giving him amazing flexibility, and using his smarts he may gain even more Wilds when he’s outclassed. He may not be a powerful fighter, but his sorcery gives him resilience and insight into the enemy’s plans. A master of deception, even at this young age, he can talk Thugs into becoming Civilians, and even eliminate Villains’ Crisis tokens.
