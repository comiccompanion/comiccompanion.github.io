Daredevil

Matt Murdock is a defender of the people, and as the Daredevil he excels at performing Heroic Actions to save the day. He is compelled to save everyone, so he’s tirelessly moving all over the conflict zone. He may get extra Movement tokens for each Civilian he rescues, saving people around the whole city in a single turn. He’s also relentless in his pursue of justice, gaining the same benefits to take out all Thugs in town, Location by Location. His Radar Senses also ensure he’s ready always ready to face whatever he finds in a new Location, gaining Heroic or Attack tokens for each Location he enters.
