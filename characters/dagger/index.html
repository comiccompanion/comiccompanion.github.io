Dagger

Dagger is a ray of heroic light. With her Lightforce Generation starting hand card she gets a free Heroic action at the start of every turn, as long as she has 3 or more cards in hand. Her bond with Cloak also allows him to heal himself if they are together. Her Lightforce Daggers hit multiple targets around her, while the influence of her Lightforce Purge can turn Thugs into Civilians, rescuing them.

Note: As these Stretch Goals are a last-minute addition due to the unprecedented success of the campaign, we don’t yet have these characters sculpted. We are working on it and we’ll update you as soon as they are approved.
