Ghost Rider (Johnny Blaze)

Johnny Blaze’s Ghost Rider brings judgement to those who commit evil. If redemption is still an option, his Sin Removal turns Thugs into Civilians. If they can’t resist his Penance Stare, however, he can wipe out all Thugs in a Location. And when things get really bad, he can simply rain hellfire across several locations!

Being the Ghost Rider comes with some perks, like the Hellfire Chain equipment which can lash out against a couple targets at once. But it’s the Hell Cycle that really lights things up, giving you a free Move. The Hell Cycle doesn’t run on gas, but on punishment, so it’s automatically recharged whenever you defeat a couple of Thugs!
