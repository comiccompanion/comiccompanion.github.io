Bullseye

Bullseye is an extremely efficient assassin, and so his plan is very straightforward: take down the Heroes. He is able to turn anything into a lethal weapon, striking with uncanny accuracy, so Heroes are never safe from his devastating attacks. He’s able to target them at adjacent Locations, or even all the way across the play area. The Threats he puts in play set up hiding places and diversions that help protect him from the Heroes. Nevertheless, you will need to band together and defeat Bullseye quickly, otherwise he’s putting you down for good.
