Pyslocke

Psylocke is an aggressive Hero, wielding her katana and psychic blade with great effectiveness. Using telepathy, she’s able to swap the previous Hero card in the Storyline with any other, giving her the support she needs (and possibly extending another Hero’s power). With a psionic blast she can stun any Villain or Henchman, keeping them from activating their BAM on the next Villain turn.
