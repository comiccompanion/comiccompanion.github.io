Shatterstar

Shatterstar was born and raised to be a fighter, and he wouldn’t have survived through the gladiatorial games in Mojoworld if he didn’t excel at it. Fast on his feet, and even faster with his blades, Shatterstar can push his body to the limit, causing vibratory shock-waves that can knock out multiple enemies around him. And of course, his healing factor comes in really handy in the combat arenas.
