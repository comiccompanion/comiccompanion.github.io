Ronan

Ronan the Accuser is a very straightforward Villain, with a simple plan: destroy the Guardians of the Galaxy. That doesn’t make him any easier to defeat, though. He will actively chase the Heroes wherever they go, attacking them relentlessly and with devastating strikes, until they’re utterly crushed.

His Threats consist of imprisoning the Heroes, so they have a hard time moving around, using elite Kree forces as Thugs, and ambushing the Heroes to try and put them down.

Ronan may be a simple man, but he’s an utterly lethal opponent. Hard to flee from, even harder to take on directly.
