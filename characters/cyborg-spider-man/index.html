Cyborg Spider Man

Cyborg Spider-Man is a powerful addition to the roster of arachnid heroes. His cybernetic body can shrug off a lot of damage, while at the same time his powerful mechanical arm can dish out an equally large amount of damage. But under all those cyber-parts still beats the heart of a Spider-Man and his Spider-Sense has not been compromised, allowing him to move away whenever a Villain corners him.

As part of his cyber-enhancements, Cyborg Spider-Man has the Sonic Cannon equipment card. It is able to clear up any negative or protective effects placed on Thugs and Civilians by blasting an adjacent Location. While recharging the Sonic Cannon is costly, one of his Hero Cards can do it automatically.
