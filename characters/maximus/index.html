Maximus

Maximus is a Villain with a chip on his shoulder and powerful psionic powers. To execute his schemes of ascension to power he manipulates the Heroes, compromising their teamwork. Whenever he BAMs, Heroes in Locations close to him must reveal a random card from their hands, being forced to perform tasks depending on the symbols on their card, running away from Maximus, adding Civilians or Thugs to their location, accelerating the Master Plan, or even attacking each other!
