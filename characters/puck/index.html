Puck

Puck is an extremely acrobatic Hero, with a unique fighting style that grants him extra Movement and Attacks. A starting hand card reflects the amazing resistance of his rubber-like compressed body, allowing him to ignore 1 damage dealt by Henchmen every Villain turn. Puck is also a brilliant tactician, which gives him the ability of swapping the Threat card in his Location with an adjacent one.
