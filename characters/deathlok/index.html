Deathlok

An unstoppable cyborg supersoldier, Deathlok carries massive weaponry, giving him substantial attacking power, and enabling him to use any Action token to attack. His cybernetic reflexes make him very agile and able to avoid damage. If he does get damaged, though, he is able to make repairs to his cybernetic body on the field. Meanwhile, his computer mind allows him to choose the cards he wants whenever he draws new ones!
