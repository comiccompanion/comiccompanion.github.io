Redwing

Redwing is Falcon’s faithful uh… Falcon Companion and he’s willing to lend his eye-in-the sky support to your Hero! Redwing is incredibly responsive and will soar quickly to where he needs to be and can perform an Attack or Heroic Action there. Working in unison with his helping wings, the Hero and Redwing can even perform simultaneous Actions each in their Location. Redwing excels as a scout, revealing the Villain’s next Master Plan cards while flying above them.
