Super Skrull

The Super-Skrull is a very adaptable and resourceful Villain, constantly using his shapeshifting abilities to mimic the Heroes. His BAM effect replicates the last Hero cards in the Storyline to fuel the Super-Skrull’s actions. In preparation for the Skrull invasion, his plan involves taking out the Heroes, leaving them completely vulnerable if he’s able to knock them all out at the same time.

With his Threats, the Super-Skrull uses seemingly innocent Civilians as Skrull spies, springing deadly traps on the Heroes. Skrull Warriors bolster his forces, while Skrull Decoys distract the Heroes, keeping them from damaging the Super-Skrull.
