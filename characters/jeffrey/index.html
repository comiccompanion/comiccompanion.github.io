Jeffrey

Jeffrey, the Land Shark, is Gwenpool’s sharp Companion, but he can go surfing with any Hero. Jeff may be cute as a button, but those teeth are sharp, and they love to do some chompin’. He can Attack in his Location or jump to an adjacent Location and Attack there. When his hunger gets really bad, he’ll discard a Civilian or Thug in his Location and any Location he enters!
