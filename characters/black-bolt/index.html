Black Bolt

As the king of the Inhumans, Black Bolt’s leadership is legendary, allowing him to distribute a couple of any Action tokens he wishes among the Heroes. By manipulating matter and energy he can protect Heroes in his Location or attack all enemies there. He’s also able to generate a hypersonic scream that can lay waste to an enemy on the other side of the play area. Black Bolt has a strong bond with Medusa, his wife, allowing him to use a card she played at any point as the previous card in the Storyline.
