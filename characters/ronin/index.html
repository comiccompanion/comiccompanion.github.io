Ronin

In these tough times, Clint Barton abandoned his Hawkeye persona, becoming the merciless Ronin. Focused on revenge, he’s able to ignore any damage directed at him. And if he does eventually get beat-up, he’s able to recover quickly as long as he stays put to patch himself up. Replacing his bow for a blade, Clint lost none of his deadliness, dealing powerful Attacks as long as he’s able to close in on the enemy.
