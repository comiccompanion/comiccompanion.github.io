Moon Girl & Devil Dinosaur

Moon Girl and Devil Dinosaur are certainly an unusual Super Hero duo. The giant T-Rex is not the only one pulling his weight, though. Moon Girl has super genius intellect, which not only grants her Wild tokens, but allows her to use any Action tokens as Wilds. But yes, riding a dinosaur into battle does bring its own set of advantages. Whenever Devil Dinosaur charges into a location without other Heroes there, he may attack for free. However, this does cause a Civilian there to (understandably) flee in terror.
