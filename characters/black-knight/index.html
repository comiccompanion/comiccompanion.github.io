Black Knight

Black Knight is a great tactician, able to mobilize all of his allies so they can position themselves to attack the enemies or rescue those in danger. While his armor may protect him from all damage, his command of science and magic doubles the Heroic Actions he uses to clear Threats. Riding his winged steed, Strider, Black Knight gets a free Move every turn (at least until he takes a damage).

Black Knight wields the Ebony Blade equipment card. Having a permanent effect that grants a free Attack action every turn may seem like a blessing, but the Ebony Blade carries a terrible blood curse, compelling Black Knight to attack someone every turn, otherwise he must discard a card himself.
