Nova

Sam Alexander loves being an agent of the Nova Corps and helping out wherever he’s needed. He’s a hero to the core, and so his deck offers many opportunities for Heroic Actions. His special cards make him very versatile, with Wild actions he can share with others, and the rare ability to zip around and perform Heroic Actions in adjacent Locations.
