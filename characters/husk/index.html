Husk

Huskâ€™s power manifests in a very unique way. She tears off her skin to reveal one of a different material underneath. She starts the game with her normal skin, but when she plays one of her Skin cards, she tears off her previous skin and gains a new effect related to her new skin. This remains in effect until she plays a different Skin card, replacing her permanent effect.

Her adamantium skin protects her from damage every Villain turn. Her fire skin gives her extra Attacks every turn. Her rubber make her stretchy so she can perform extra Heroic Actions.

Her stone skin gives her the toughness needed to defeat a Thug or rescue a Civilian every turn. Finally, when she needs to recover, she can go back to her normal human skin in order to draw 1 card.

To give her extra control of her abilities, her Omni-Morph cards allow her to swap faceup Skin cards in the Storyline or rearrange her deck.
