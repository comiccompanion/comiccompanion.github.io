Emma Frost (P5)

It didn’t take much to seduce Emma Frost with the power of the Phoenix Force. It enhances her mind-control powers, assigning Crisis tokens to all Heroes around her. Any Hero under her influence is forced to play their card randomly on their turn. She’s able to look deep into each Hero’s psyche to turn their sins against them, permanently removing the top card from their deck. Her Threat cards fuel her hunger for power, as she gains Health for each Threat around her. These Threats reflect her enhanced telepathic ability, each preventing the Heroes from using a different type of action, clouding their minds, or draining their energy.
