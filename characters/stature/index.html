Stature

Due to her ability to increase her size to impressive proportions, Stature has an increased starting hand, beginning the game with an extra card in hand. Using Pym Particles, she can get extra Attack or Heroic tokens. When in trouble she can shrink to avoid damage. Conversely, she can grow to a gigantic size, covering great distances and following it up with a powerful attack. Due to her relative inexperience, though, her gigantic size can be a liability. While she can attack for free whenever she enters a location without other Heroes, if any other Hero enters her location they must discard 1 card.

Stature’s Suit is what enables her to change her size so drastically. Using this Equipment card, she’s able to draw her Grow or Shrink cards at the moment she really needs them.
