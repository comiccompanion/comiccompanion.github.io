Squirrel Girl

While Squirrel Girl may seem like a silly character, she’s a Hero the Villain would be wise to take seriously. Her sunny disposition makes her extremely active, and just because she’s upbeat doesn’t mean she won’t beat down on the enemies. Her keen vision lets her see what’s coming in the Masterplan deck, and even alter it, while her common sense may even accelerate the Heroes’ victory. But don’t laugh when she summons her army of squirrels to swarm every Location. One squirrel may not do much, but an army of them can soften the enemy up a bit.
