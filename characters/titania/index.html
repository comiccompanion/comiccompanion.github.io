Titania

Titania is a rough-and-tumble adversary. She fights hard, she fights dirty, and she can take it as good as she can dish out, with Master Plan cards that allow her to move and BAM twice in a row, and Threats that give her extra resistance and additional attacks. Whenever she KO’s a Hero, they take a KO token. Whenever the Heroes would defeat her, she takes a Crisis token and gets right back up (only difference is that now she’s even angrier, so she deals extra damage for each Crisis token she has). This all-out brawl goes on until someone runs out of cards. At this point, players compare the number of KO and Crisis tokens to determine the winner!

Players can also choose to add Titania’s partner, Absorbing Man, as a Challenge. Once the first Threat is cleared, the Absorbing Man Threat is placed in Titania’s location. This Henchman can deal out damage across several locations and is remarkably hard to take down.
