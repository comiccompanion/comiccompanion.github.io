Jean Grey

Jean Grey’s mind powers are truly second to none. Using her telepathy, she’s able to influence other to make the most of it. On her turn, she may swap the previous Hero card for any other in the Storyline. This not only allows her to have all the most useful actions available to her, but there may be other effects greatly influenced by such a change. And her mind powers also allow her to use telekinesis to perform Heroic and Attack actions in any Location around her.
