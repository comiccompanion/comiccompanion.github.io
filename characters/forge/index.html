Forge

Forge is a resourceful Hero who’s as in touch with nature as he is with technology. Using his intuitive genius, he’s able to double any Heroic actions he spends on clearing a Threat. On the spiritual side, he possesses a spirit sight that allows him to project himself into another Location to perform Attacks or Heroic actions there.
