Okoye

Okoye is Dora Milaje, an elite force of women warriors who protect the King of Wakanda, a task in which she excels. Having trained all her life for her role, Okoye has become a master combatant whether armed or not. Her vibranium spear allows her to fight enemies in adjacent Locations, while her Dora Milaje training enables to her to shield other Heroes, taking damage for them. Last but not least, as an accomplished strategist, Okoye can sometimes manipulate her deck to her advantage.
