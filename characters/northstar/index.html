Northstar

Whether running or flying, Northstar is extremely mobile, with lots of Move actions available to him. A starting hand card even grants him the ability to fly away with another Hero in tow, moving them along with him whenever he Moves. By using an extreme form of photokinesis, Northstar can generate concussive blasts out of his body, attacking multiple enemies across several Locations. Alpha Flight is a close-knit team, working very well together. Northstar, Puck, and Guardian all have an Alpha Team card that grants them specific extra Action tokens for each of those cards in the Storyline. Northstar gains extra Heroic Action tokens.
