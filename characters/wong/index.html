Wong

Wong is not only a master of the mystic arts, but a consummate martial artist, doubling Attack symbols he gets from the previous Hero’s card. His use of sorcery is often done in support of others. He is able to open portals that take him to any location, also allowing all Heroes with him to go along. When he projects magical protective shields, he allows all Heroes around him to ignore the first damage they would take. That’s why everybody loves Wong!
