Sunfire

Sunfire is able to turn all forms of radiation into hot plasma. The hot air from this power allows him to fly, granting him great mobility. He can absorb solar radiation around him, for each Location that is free from Civilians and Thugs, Sunfire is able to gain an Attack token to use as he will. But most devastating are the sun flares he can blast out onto an adjacent Location, damaging everything there.
