Omega Red

The Carbonadium tentacles implanted in Omega Red allow him not only to attack multiple Heroes in adjacent Locations, but he’s also able to absorb their life force, healing and boosting himself. Infecting Heroes with lethal spores, he’s able to damage them and hinder their effectiveness by keeping them from drawing new cards if they already have 3 in hand. With his Threats, he ensures that every time he BAMs, Thugs or Civilians are added to every Location. This makes Overflows a constant danger, and when they happen, Heroes can be seriously damaged.
