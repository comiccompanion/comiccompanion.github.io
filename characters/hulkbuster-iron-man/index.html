Hulkbuster Iron Man

To take on the Hulk, Tony Stark brings his most powerful armor, the Hulkbuster. This behemoth was specially designed to counter the Hulk, with shockwave emitters that can either be used for attack or to stun the Villain or Henchman, cancelling their BAM effect. His Starting Hand card engages Damage Mitigation Mode, allowing Iron Man to flip that card instead of being KO’d. The Hulkbuster can even pummel an enemy into the ground, pinning them down so they can’t move.

The Hulkbuster comes equipped with a couple single-use cards. The Deflector Shields prevents any damage, while the Omnibeam fires a single blast that deals an absurd amount of damage, but KO’s the Hulkbuster.
