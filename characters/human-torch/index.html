Human Torch

Johnny Storm is a very mobile and hot-headed Hero, with a Pyrogenesis starting hand card that allows him to consider any Heroic symbols as Attacks. Or he can simply go Nova, dealing damage to everything in his Location (even himself), and then adding an extra Attack on top of it! His Teamwork cards add Move tokens to the Team card.
