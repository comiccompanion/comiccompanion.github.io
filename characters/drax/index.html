Drax

Drax the Destroyer is driven by a single desire: revenge. His entire existence revolves around combat, making him a very different kind of Hero. His deck is made almost entirely of fighting actions. When he flies into a rage he does so quite literally, dropping on the next Location to pummel down on an enemy.
