Snowbird

Snowbird can assume the shape of any sort of arctic animal, easily adapting to any challenge by gaining access to extra Wild actions. A starting hand card grants her postcognition, which gives her Wild tokens whenever one of her cards is turned facedown. As an ancient mystic being, Snowbird has incredible resistance to outside influences, so nothing can force her to play her cards randomly. As a member of Alpha Flight, she can benefit from Alpha Team cards played by other members, gaining extra Move or Heroic Action tokens.
