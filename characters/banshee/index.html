Banshee

Using his supersonic screams, Banshee is able to fly around and disrupt the plans of any Villain. His screams produce stunning waves that can keep a Villain or Henchman from using their BAM on the next Villain turn. He can also project a sonic blast to deal a heavy blow to enemies in adjacent locations.
