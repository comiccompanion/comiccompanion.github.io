Jubilee

Jubilee may seem like a harmless kid, with her colorful look, sunny disposition, and sparkly mutant abilities, but she’s as capable as any X-Man. Raising a psionic shield around her, she can protect herself from any effects caused by the Villain’s BAM. And those sparks she generates can be used to great effect to attack multiple targets around her at the same time.
