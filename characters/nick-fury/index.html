Nick Fury

Nick Fury may be the director of S.H.I.E.L.D. but that doesn’t mean he’s comfortable with a desk job. He’s not a front-line fighter, but he’s always in the thick of things, rescuing people and making sure his operatives perform at peak capacity. His Master Tactician ability allows other Heroes to immediately perform the Actions he needs them to.
