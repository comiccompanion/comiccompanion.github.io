Jessica Jones

Jessica Jones is extremely strong and is able to perform her share of Heroic Actions, but it’s truly her investigative skills and strong willpower that make her an invaluable Hero in the fight against Villains. Using her PI abilities, she is able to reveal Masterplan cards and anticipate the baddies’ actions, and when endangered by the Villains powers, she can return cards to her hand, preventing her from being KO’d.
