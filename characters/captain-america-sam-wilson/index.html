Captain America (Sam Wilson)

As Captain America, Sam Wilson’s leadership inspires his teammates, granting them extra Heroic and Attack tokens. His military training allows him to double the Attack symbols on the previous Hero card on the Storyline, and he also gains extra Attacks from his expert use of Captain America’s Shield. On top of that, his wings provide Sam with amazing mobility, doubling each Move he performs.

Taking on Captain America’s Shield was not an easy thing for Sam, but this amazing piece of equipment comes in very handy. It can be used to either attack an adjacent location or to protect Heroes in your location. One of Sam’s Hero cards allows him to recharge the shield for free, keeping it always at the ready.
