Maria Hill

Nick Fury’s right hand woman, Maria Hill eventually ascends to become Director of S.H.I.E.L.D., giving her the control to have all Heroes perform an out of turn Action while she heals herself. She is exceptionally well trained, knowing when to Attack and when to duck out of the way when the Villain comes around. She’s also a master spy, able to look at the next Master Plan card and place it at the bottom of the deck if she deems it necessary. But most of all, her dedication is unparalleled, leading her to sacrifice cards from her hand to immediately perform any additional Actions she requires.
