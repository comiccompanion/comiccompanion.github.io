Archangel

Flying on metal wings, Archangel is always on the move, being a great ally to help move other Heroes around. His techno organic wings can act as a shield, keeping him safe from all attacks while he has them wrapped around him. They can also be used on the offensive, firing off metal feathers into adjacent Locations, with devastating effect.
